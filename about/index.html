This is the website of Chad 'Piffey' Anderson. I am a cybersecurity researcher and photographer who enjoys writing. This page aims to be a curation of mixed writings on subjects I enjoy as well as a place to showcase my photography. You can find out more on my about page.

about

me


Hello, I am Chad ‘Piffey’ Anderson, a security researcher and photographer living and working in Seattle, Washington. Currently, I work at DomainTools on the research and development team.

My cybersecurity work involves the analysis and creation of infrastructure data sets used in the distillation of data into threat intelligence for analysts and researchers around the world. I enjoy writing automation and tooling to further the success of security and research teams looking to analyze cybercriminal groups and nation state threat actors. With a background steeped in DevOps, I find the crisp automation of infrastructure as code and the use of cloud native architectures to be a theme through what I create and am influenced into researching. Lastly, I also enjoy work in the offensive security space and malware reverse engineering as I feel both greatly inform all of the other work.

My photographic practice can best be aligned with the tradition of American street photography where I explore our modern daily social life mostly in the city of Seattle. I strongly feel that this public visual language is essential to documenting the pinnacle changes of our time in a timeless, honest way. Curiosity and a humorous outlook on the world around me drives my attempts to represent what I see in a beautiful, meaningful way I can share. I work with traditional methods in my Seattle darkroom where I develop my own negatives and create silver gelatin prints.

Lastly, I enjoy writing on many topics and that is a lot of what you will see on this site coupled with a way to show my photographs. Previously, I kept all of these things in disparate places, but decided that as they all influence each other in my life I would like them to be combined in a single space.
